Hi and welcome to origami thing!
This is a tool that you can design your very own origami paper in!
I got inspired by all the beautiful traditional japanese patterns you can
find printed either on paper or on fabric, would'nt it be fun to create your
own and fold it into a little origami?

Here is how you do it:
You see the blank canvas? Feel free to use all your creativity on it!
Choose a color from the rainbow waterfall, choose your brush and scale
your pattern to your desired size!
Save it, and you get a little surprise origami instruction with your
pattern PDF. Print it, and cut off the excess of your paper so you have
a square. And now -> Happy folding!
