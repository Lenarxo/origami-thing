Hi and welcome to origami thing!
This is a tool that you can design your very own origami paper in!
I got inspired by all the beautiful traditional japanese patterns you can
find either printed on paper or on fabric, would'nt it be fun to create your
own and fold it into a little origami?

Here is how you do it:
Choose a color from the waterfall, choose your brush,
draw on the canvas and scale your pattern to your desired size!
Save it, and you will get a little surprise origami instruction with your
pattern PDF. Print it, cut off the excess to end up with a square.
And now; Happy folding!
